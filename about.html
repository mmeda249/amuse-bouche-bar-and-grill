Our Story

Where It All Began

● ● ●

Amuse Bouche developed from the minds of Founder Cristiano Carletti and his cousin Executive Chef Mateo Rossi. Having grown up in the coastal Italian town of Portofino, they have been surrounded by Mediterranean delicacies for many years. A love of travelling further inspired them to marry their culture and food adventures and create a unique dining experience.

Blending rustic and fine dining, Amuse Bouche delivers exquisite seasonal Mediterranean fusion cuisine from Italy, Greece, Morocco, France, Tunisia, Croatia, Lebanon and Spain. Our restaurant features menus crafted from the freshest and highest quality ingredients, tastiest beverages and gorgeous decor that will transport you to straight to the Med.
