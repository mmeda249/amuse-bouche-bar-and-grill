Our Story

● ● ●

French for “amuse the mouth,” an amuse bouche is a small, delicious bite of food chefs serve to welcome guests before the appetizer, exciting their palates for the meal to come.

Amuse Bouche Bistro has been serving exquisite bites and Mediterranean meals crafted by renowned chefs for over a decade.
