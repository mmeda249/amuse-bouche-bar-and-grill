Our Story

● ● ●

French for “to amuse the mouth,” an amuse bouche is a delicious bite of food chefs prepare to welcome and delight guests , exciting their pallettes for the meal to come.

Amuse Bouche Bar & Grill has been serving exquisite bites and Mediterranean meals crafted by renowned chefs for over a decade.
